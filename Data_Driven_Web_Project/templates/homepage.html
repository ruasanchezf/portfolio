Site Purpose

The purpose of our side is to provide a navigable source for information of specific or collective school shooting information in the U.S. K-12 public school system in order to recognize patterns in school shootings and promote discussion with the vision of finding possible solutions.

About Us

We are a team of three college students who want to raise awareness about the increasing problem of gun violence in our country. Since we are students, we wanted to focus on inciting discussion about incidents that occur in educational institutions. Those incidents have a direct impact on us as students. We hope to bring awareness to this information so that our community leaders can make informed decisions on safety regulations to prevent the increasing amount of gun violence.
